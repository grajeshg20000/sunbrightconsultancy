Oracle BPM 11g Modeling course has been designed to train the participants about using the BPM in modeling, simulating, implementing, playing, deploying and monitoring the business processes. The business process behaviors would be demonstrated for simulation, result analysis and process optimization. This Oracle BPM Training course focuses on creating the process models and running simulations, implementing and playing simple processes, using BAM to create the real-time dashboards, run-time modification of business rules, viewing the sample analytic data by using the BAM and Business Process Workspace.
