The certification authenticates a Project Manager’s mastery and experience of the agile processes and principles. An Agile qualified practitioner has the skills and knowledge of both Agile practices and Waterfall and is capable to guide teams on Agile projects. Being certified in Agile brings more credibility; a professional confirms their agile proficiency to corporates looking to implement the agile methods. Koenig’s Agile Project Management professionals desire to assist qualified Project Managers to become certified.We have created an interactive course with the intention of preparing Project Managers for the exam of PMI Agile Certified Practitioner (PMI-ACP)®. After completing this course, students will be able to demonstrate employers their high level of professionalism in agile principles, tools practices, and techniques and students will also be able to enhance their professional adaptability in project management techniques and tools. Course Objectives: Comprehend about Agile. Comprehend Scrum. Comprehend Domains of Agile Practices. Comprehend Extreme Programming. Comprehend Lean Kanban. Comprehend Risk Management. Comprehend Communication Management. Comprehend Soft skills.
