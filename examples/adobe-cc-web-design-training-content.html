The Adobe Photoshop CC course offered by Koenig Solutions provides a comprehensive training to participants who are looking for a breakthrough in their careers in the field of design and development. Being the successor to Photoshop CS6, Photoshop CC is a part of the Creative Cloud suite, offered by Adobe. New features in this software include Smart Sharpen, and a Camera Shake Reduction facility to reduce blur as a result of a shaky camera. Other features include improved Content-Aware tools, updated Adobe Camera Raw (8.0), customizable Rounded Rectangles, two advanced blur tools (path blur and spin blur), and a high-end focus mask facility which aids users in selecting parts of images depending on the level of focus on them; all of which are directed towards increasing the speed of the application.
